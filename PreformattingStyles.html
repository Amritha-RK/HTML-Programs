What is paragraph?

A paragraph is a group of related
sentenses about a single topic. The
topic is one, and only one idea.




A paragraph has three main parts:

        1. Topic Sentence: It is the first sentence in a 
        paragraph. It names the topic and tells what 
        the paragraph will explain.

        2. Supporting Sentences: They are the middle sentences in a paragraph.
        They give examples or other details about the topic.

        3. Concluding Sentences: It is the last sentence in a 
        paragraph. It is often repeat the topic sentence in
        different words or summarize the main points.
