Sunrise

Sunrise (or sunup) is the moment when the upper rim of the Sun appears on the horizon in the morning.
The term can also refer to the entire process of the solar disk crossing the horizon.

Although the Sun appears to "rise" from the horizon, it is actually the Earth's motion that causes the
Sun to appear. The illusion of a moving Sun results from Earth observers being in a rotating reference
frame;this apparent motion caused many cultures to have mythologies and religions built around
the geocentric model, which prevailed until astronomer Nicolaus Copernicus formulated his
heliocentric model in the 16th century
